<h2>Installation</h2>
Install the Localist Calendar for Drupal module by clicking "Modules" then "Install new module" from your Drupal admin. Add the module via the Drupal module directory, or by uploading the files manually to your server. After becoming a Localist customer, use the module to connect to your calendar and enable event listing features.

<h2>Usage</h2>
<h3>How do I use the module?</h3>
After installing the **Localist Calendar for Drupal** module, you can begin setting up new widgets in the Localist Calendar section of your Drupal administration.

To begin, click <em>Configuration</em> in the navigation bar and click <em>Localist Calendar</em> in the System section. Paste in your <em>Localist Calendar's URL</em> and click <em>Configure</em>. Define your criteria, choose a name for the widget, then click Save as New Block. Once the block is added, you can add it to a page, post, or theme in the appropriate areas of Drupal by clicking <em>Structure</em>, then <em>Blocks</em> and scrolling down to find the block you created.
